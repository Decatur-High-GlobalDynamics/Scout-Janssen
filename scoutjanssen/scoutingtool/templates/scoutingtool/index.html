2020 - Infinite Recharge

-March 6th through 7th: Team 4026 competed at the Georgia FIRST® Dalton District Event. After a rough first day, the team bounced back, making it to Semifinals after being picked by the 3rd place team for playoffs, where we were knocked out by the eventual tournament winners. Though we did not manage to win the tournament, we did manage to take home the Judge's Award!

-March 20th: After one official competition, the team was disappointed to find out that FIRST® had suspended, and later cancelled, the remainder of the INFINITE RECHARGE season, with the decision to replay a modified verson of INFINITE RECHARGE as the 2021 season game. Though we are all disappointed, we have learned how to take the situation with our heads held high, and are looking forward to the 2021 season!
